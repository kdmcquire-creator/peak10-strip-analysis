COMPOSITE HEDGE SIGNAL — FULL HISTORY WITH REALIZED-VS-STRIP LINES
Custom signal formula (0–100, Peak 10 internal): Strip pct rank ×30 + Regime (HC/HB/LC/LB) ×20 + 3-mo momentum ×15 + OVX implied vol ×20 (or strip vol ×15 if OVX unavailable) + COT prod. net short ×15. Higher = stronger case for hedging now. Colored lines (right axis, inverted): Formula: (Cn_at_T0 − realized_C1) / realized_C1 × 100. Denominator = realized spot (symmetric: ±$20 from same price = same %). Positive = hedge captured premium over market (good). Negative = market came in above locked-in price (bad). Inverted axis: positive (good hedge) plots downward. High signal + line going down = methodology validation.
